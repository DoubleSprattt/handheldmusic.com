Korg M01D (2013)

3DS version of the M01 — added SD/MIDI export, more polyphony and expanded soundset.

Back

Overview

Korg M01D is a 3DS music workstation based on the Korg M1. It expanded the DS version with higher polyphony, more sounds, SD save support and the ability to export MIDI — turning the handheld into a practical sketchpad for desktop production. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

Key features

  • 24-voice polyphony and over 300 sounds
  • Song data + MIDI export to SD card
  • Sequencer, mixer, and expanded sample pages
  • StreetPass/online sharing of song data (where supported)

Why M01D matters

The M01D’s MIDI export and SD support enabled composers to move sketches from handheld to desktop DAWs — a practical bridge lacking in most DS-era music titles. Its faithful M1 soundset and improved polyphony made it a popular 3DS eShop offering when released in 2013. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}

Practical tips

  • Use MIDI export to transfer patterns to your DAW for full production.
  • Take advantage of SD card saves to preserve many song iterations.
  • Explore the additional sound pages — many classic M1 presets are available.
